What should I do if someone I don't know messages me online?

Never reply to messages from strangers online! Tell a trusted adult like your parents or teacher right away. Remember: Online strangers might not be who they say they are.

Is it okay to share my password with my best friend?

No way! Keep your passwords super secret - don't share them with anyone, not even your best friends! Your passwords are like the keys to your online home. Keep them safe!

What personal information should I never share online?

Never share your full name, home address, phone number, school name, passwords, or pictures without your parents' permission. These are your private details that should stay private!

What should I do if I see something scary or confusing online?

If you see something that makes you feel uncomfortable, scared, or confused - stop looking right away and tell a grown-up you trust. It's always better to ask for help!

How do I know if a website is safe to visit?

Look for the padlock symbol in the address bar, check that the website address starts with "https://", and always ask a grown-up before visiting new websites. Safe websites protect your information!

What is cyberbullying and what should I do if it happens?

Cyberbullying is when someone is mean or unkind to you online. If this happens, don't respond to the bully. Save the evidence, block the person, and tell a trusted adult right away. Remember: It's never your fault if you're bullied!
